Penne Alla Vodka

A creamy and tasty tomato based pasta sauce elevated with vodka.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Servings of your choice of short pasta (Penne perferred)
  • 2 medium cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 small shallot, minced
  • 1/4 medium onion, minced
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup of vodka
  • 1-2 teaspoons red pepper flakes
  • 2-3 sprigs of fresh basil
  • salt
  • (Optional) Parmigiano Reggiano Cheese

Steps

  1. Heat oil in pan on medium heat.
  2. Cook onion and shallot until slightly translucent. Add garlic when translucent.
  3. Add tomato paste and cook until tomato paste turns slightly darker red.
  4. Add in vodka. Cook until all the vodka is evaporated.
  5. Add in red chili flakes.
  6. Turn heat to medium-low, add in heavy cream and milk.
  7. Then finish the sauce off with butter.
  8. Mix in your pasta. Using pasta water is needed to reconstitute sauce.
  9. Plate and finish with basil on top.
  10. (Optional) Grate some cheese on top before serving.
  11. Enjoy!
