Cacio E Pepe

Cacio E Pepe is a simple, but filling pasta made with cheese and black pepper.

Ingredients

  • 1-2 Servings of your choice of long pasta (Spaghetti or Linguini preferred)
  • 1/3 Cup of Parmigiano Reggiano Cheese, Very Finely Grated
  • 1 1/2 Teaspoon of Whole or Crushed Black Peppercorns

Steps

  1. Toast peppercorns in an pan without oil or anything for a few minutes until fragrant.
  2. Salt and boil pasta water.
  3. When your pasta water comes to a rolling boil, cook pasta until al dente.
  4. Half-way through cooking the pasta, ladel some pasta water to cover black peppercorns in the other pan.
  5. Take another ladel of pasta water and mix it into your bowl of finely grated Parmigiano Reggiano Cheese. Mix well until it becomes a liquid paste. Set aside for now.
  6. Once pasta is al dente, trasnfer into pan with the black peppercorns and pasta water.
  7. Turn off heat and mix in the liquid cheesy paste.
  8. Mix well until emulsified with pasta. If the sauce starts to break (the oil separating from the cheese and pasta water), add a little bit of pasta water and start mixing lightly again.
  9. Plate your pasta, and finish off with a little bit of freshly cracked black pepper and a dash of cheese.
  10. Enjoy!
